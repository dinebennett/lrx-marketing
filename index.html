ABOUT US

WHO ARE WE?
LRX Marketing is a Port Elizabeth based business competing in the South African market for fans (air moving devices) and accessories.

WHAT DO WE DO?
LRX Marketing sources, imports and supplies fans (air moving devices) and accessories to organizations distributing and trading fans in the South African market.
